Principles

Two books that deeply impacted me were Meditations by Marcus Aurelius and Principles by Ray Dalio. I thought I should keep track of mine and make sure I live by them.

1. Play Everyday

2. Spend more time creating than consuming

3. Evolve or die.

4. Dream big and dream often

5. Strive to punch above your weight
